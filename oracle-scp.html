What We Deliver

SCP Cloud Capabilities

Swift Technologies deploys Oracle SCP with pre-tuned AI models and industry-specific planning parameters for faster time-to-value.

Demand Planning

Statistical forecasting enhanced by AI demand sensing from POS data, social signals, and market indicators for up to 40% better accuracy.

Supply Planning

Multi-echelon inventory optimization that automatically calculates safety stock, reorder points, and replenishment orders across your network.

S&OP Planning

Structured sales & operations planning process with executive dashboards, scenario modeling, and consensus planning workflows.

Integrated Business Planning

Connect financial plans with operational plans in a single platform — bridging the gap between CFO forecasts and supply chain realities.

Replenishment Planning

Automated store and DC replenishment with Min/Max, reorder point, and days-of-supply policies dynamically adjusted by AI.

Market Planning

New product introduction planning, product lifecycle management, and promotional lift modeling to align sales execution with supply.
