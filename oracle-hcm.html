What We Deliver

HCM Cloud Capabilities

Swift Technologies HCM implementations are built on 15+ years of global HR transformation experience across manufacturing, financial services, and technology companies.

Core HR & Global Data Model

Single worker record across 200+ countries with local statutory compliance, org management, and self-service HR transactions for managers and employees.

Global Payroll

Multi-country payroll with local tax, benefits, and statutory deductions — processed in a single system with real-time gross-to-net calculations and automatic regulatory updates.

Talent Management

AI-powered recruiting, performance management, succession planning, compensation benchmarking, and career development pathways in one connected talent suite.

Workforce Management

Time and labor tracking, absence management, shift scheduling, and labor cost allocation — integrated directly with payroll for zero-touch timesheet processing.

Learning & Development

AI-recommended learning paths, compliance training tracking, skills gap analysis, and external content library integration via Oracle Learning Cloud.

HR Analytics

Pre-built workforce KPI dashboards — headcount trends, attrition risk scoring, pay equity analysis, and DEI reporting with drill-down to individual worker data.
