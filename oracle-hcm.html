HCM Cloud Capabilities

Swift IT Services HCM implementations bring deep HR transformation experience across all major industries.

Core HR & Global Data Model
Single worker record across multiple regions with local statutory compliance, org management, and self-service HR transactions.
Global Payroll
Multi-country payroll with local tax, benefits, and statutory deductions processed in a single system with automatic regulatory updates.
Talent Management
AI-powered recruiting, performance management, succession planning, and compensation benchmarking in one connected talent suite.
Workforce Management
Time and labor tracking, absence management, shift scheduling, and labor cost allocation integrated directly with payroll.
Learning & Development
AI-recommended learning paths, compliance training, skills gap analysis, and external content library integration.
HR Analytics
Pre-built workforce KPI dashboards — headcount trends, attrition risk scoring, pay equity analysis, and DEI reporting.
