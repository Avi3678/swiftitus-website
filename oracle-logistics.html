Transportation Management
Optimize Every Move, Every Mode
Oracle Transportation Management Cloud provides full multi-modal TMS capabilities — from carrier procurement and load building to rate management and freight audit. Embedded AI optimizes routes and consolidates loads to reduce freight spend by 10-15%.
  • Multi-modal: TL, LTL, parcel, ocean, air, rail
  • AI-driven load optimization and carrier selection
  • Real-time shipment tracking and event management
  • Freight audit, payment processing, and carrier scorecards
Warehouse Management
Drive Warehouse Efficiency to New Levels
Oracle Warehouse Management Cloud delivers advanced directed work, task interleaving, and labor management to maximize warehouse throughput while minimizing errors and operating costs across your entire distribution network.
  • Directed putaway, pick, pack, and ship workflows
  • Labor management with engineered labor standards
  • Wave management, slotting, and cartonization
  • IoT device and robotics integration (Locus, 6 River, Fetch)
Global Trade Management
Navigate Complex Global Trade Compliance
Oracle Global Trade Management Cloud automates trade compliance processes including export controls, import duty management, denied party screening, and country-of-origin determination — keeping your shipments moving without compliance risk.
  • Denied party and export control screening (OFAC, BIS)
  • Automated customs documentation and filing
  • Duty drawback management and FTZ tracking
  • Country-of-origin determination and ECCN classification
