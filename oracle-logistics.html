What We Deliver

Logistics Cloud Capabilities

Swift Technologies brings deep logistics domain expertise across manufacturing, retail, and 3PL industries for Oracle Logistics implementations.

🚛

Transportation Management

AI-optimized load planning, carrier selection, multi-modal freight execution, and freight audit & payment — reducing transportation costs by up to 15%.

🏪

Warehouse Management

RF-directed workflows, wave planning, labor management, slotting optimization, and real-time inventory accuracy across single or multi-site warehouse networks.

🌐

Global Trade Management

Automated export/import compliance, trade agreement duty optimization, denied party screening, and AES/ABI filing for 150+ countries.

🅿️

Yard Management

Real-time trailer visibility, automated gate check-in/out, dock appointment scheduling, and yard jockey task management.

🚗

Fleet Management

Private fleet management with driver dispatch, route optimization, ELD compliance, and total cost of ownership tracking for your owned assets.

📡

IoT & Visibility

Real-time shipment tracking, temperature monitoring, event alerts, and customer-facing delivery ETA notifications via IoT sensor integration.
