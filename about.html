Our Story

Built for the Enterprise. Driven by Innovation.

Swift Technologies was founded in 2009 with a singular mission: to make enterprise technology work harder for the businesses that depend on it. What started as a boutique Oracle consulting firm in Dallas, Texas has grown into a global IT services powerhouse serving 500+ clients across 40+ countries.

As cloud computing matured and AI moved from research labs into production environments, we invested aggressively in building proprietary AI capabilities alongside our deep Oracle expertise. Today, Swift Technologies is recognized as a leader at the intersection of AI and enterprise cloud — a combination that gives our clients a genuine competitive edge.

Our 1,200+ certified consultants span Oracle, AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and emerging AI technologies. We operate delivery centers across North America, Europe, India, and Southeast Asia, giving clients the global scale and local expertise they need to execute complex transformation programs without compromise.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These six principles aren't aspirational posters — they're the operating system that governs every client engagement, hiring decision, and product investment we make.

01

Client Obsession

Every decision starts with one question: does this create value for our client? We measure our success by the business outcomes our clients achieve, not by the hours we bill or the technologies we deploy.

02

Innovation with Purpose

We embrace emerging technologies because they solve real problems — not because they're trending. Every AI model, every cloud architecture, every automation must demonstrate clear, measurable business value.

03

Radical Transparency

We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. When a project is at risk, we say so early. When a proposed approach has flaws, we flag them before the contract is signed — not after.

04

Excellence Without Compromise

We maintain rigorous quality standards across every engagement regardless of size. A 50-person implementation gets the same architectural scrutiny and delivery discipline as a 500-person global rollout.

05

Diverse Perspectives

Our 1,200+ team spans 35 nationalities. We believe diverse backgrounds produce better solutions, more creative problem-solving, and an inclusive culture where everyone brings their full capability to work.

06

Continuous Learning

Technology never stops evolving, and neither do we. Every Swift Technologies employee has a dedicated learning budget, and certifications are celebrated as loudly as client wins.
