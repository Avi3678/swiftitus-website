Product Development
Accelerate Innovation from Idea to Launch
Oracle Product Development Cloud connects R&D, engineering, and supply chain teams on a single platform — eliminating the handoff delays that slow time-to-market. Embedded AI recommends design alternatives and flags supply risks before they become problems.
  • Idea management and stage-gate approval workflows
  • BOM management with engineering change orders
  • CAD integration (SolidWorks, AutoCAD, Creo)
  • Supplier co-development portal and joint design
Quality Management
Build Quality In — Not Inspect It Out
Oracle Quality Management Cloud provides a closed-loop quality system that connects design, manufacturing, and supplier quality processes. Track nonconformances, execute CAPAs, and maintain complete audit trails from a single system.
  • FMEA and control plan management
  • Supplier quality scorecards and audit management
  • Complaint management and CAPA workflow tracking
  • Integration with manufacturing execution systems (MES)
Product Hub
Single Source of Truth for Product Master Data
Oracle Product Hub Cloud provides the authoritative product master data repository — harmonizing item data, specifications, and attributes across ERP, SCM, and CX systems to eliminate costly errors from inconsistent product data.
  • Item master data governance and approval workflow
  • Multi-site and multi-org item management
  • Attribute inheritance and data quality validation rules
  • Publish-and-subscribe to downstream systems
